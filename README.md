#Coffee-Shop-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)

## Project Objective 

The Coffee Shop wants to create an 6 monhts sales report for 2023.So that,the owner of the coffee 
shop can understand their customers and grow more sales in upcoming 6 months.

## Dataset Used
-<a href="https://github.com/NANDAKUMAR2124/Coffee_Shop_Sales_Report/blob/main/Coffee%20Shop%20Sales.xlsx">Coffee Shop Data</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- Total sales of months.
- Find a pick hours of sales.
- Find a pick days of sales in the week.
- Find a fast moving products transactions.
- which products moving less transactions.
- Make Top 15 products Revenue and Transactions.
- Make transactions of products by week and hour.

  ## Process
  - Verify data for any missing values and remove errors.
  - Made sure data is consistent abd clean with respict to data type,data format and values used.
  - Created pivot tables according to the questions asked.
  - Merge all pivot tables into one dashboard and apply slicer to make dynamic.

 ## Project insight
  The dashboard shows consistent revenue growth from January to June, indicating improving business performance across all locations. Customer demand is strongest on weekdays, especially Monday, Thursday, and       Friday, suggesting workday-driven traffic. Morning hours (7–10 AM) generate the highest transactions, highlighting the importance of breakfast and commuter-focused offerings. Coffee dominates all product          categories, with espresso and brewed coffee leading sales, making them key drivers of revenue.

  ## Final Conclusion
 
  The dashboard shows strong and improving performance across all coffee shop locations, driven mainly by weekday and morning customer traffic. Revenue is steadily increasing month over month, with coffee—          especially espresso and brewed varieties—being the primary contributor to sales. Focusing on peak morning hours, weekday demand, and top-performing products will help sustain growth and maximize overall           profitability.
